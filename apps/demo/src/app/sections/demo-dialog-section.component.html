Dialog

Centered modal with backdrop. Close via Escape, backdrop click, or programmatically. Dialogs animate from the trigger button (scale-from-origin, ng-zorro style).

Without trigger: dialog opens with center-only animation (no scale-from-trigger).

Content-scoped dialog: opens only in the main content area (not over header/sidebar). Clicks on header or sidebar do not close it; backdrop and Escape still do.
